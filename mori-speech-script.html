School Festival06

I made a cash register app for our school festival.

The subject changed — from rivers to software — but his words stayed with me.

After I entered this school, I found a problem I could solve. At our school festival, one shop could not easily count their money and sales. So I made a cash register app.

Design for People07

I designed every detail for the users.

I did not only make it work.

I added a long-press button, so people do not pay by mistake. I added a sales graph, so the shop can see what sells well. Every detail came from one question: how can this help people?

A Hard Lesson08

The problem was not the code. It was how people used it.

But the app did not solve everything. The problem was not a bug.

People give and take cash very fast, but my app made them stop and take out a phone. Good functions do not always make a good product. A product must fit people's actions.
